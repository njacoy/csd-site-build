## HERO

<!-- section: hero-headline -->
Every Child Should Have the Opportunity to Reach Their Fullest Potential
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: hero-body-1 -->
We believe every child with autism deserves a future defined by possibility, not limitations. For too long, autism services have been emotionally frustrating, confusing, and complicated: hard to access, overly clinical, and lacking the human connection families so desperately need.

## OUR STORY

<!-- section: story-eyebrow -->
Our Story
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: story-heading -->
Born from Belief, Built on Success
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: story-body-1 -->
CSD began in response to a need.
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: story-body-2 -->
In 2012, when Kaiser Permanente urgently had to provide ABA therapy services for 80 families in Northern California, a small but determined team said yes—and figured out the "how" together. That scrappy, all-hands-on-deck spirit of serving families, no matter what the challenge, still pulses through everything we do.
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: story-body-3 -->
We've grown up since then. From our beginnings in one Vallejo, California, clinic with a handful of passionate clinicians, we now serve thousands of families nationwide through in-home, center-based, school-based and virtual immersion therapy programs.
<!-- /section -->

<!-- section: story-body-4 -->
Today, our innovative approach to therapy is more advanced than ever, but our heart center remains the same.
<!-- /section -->
