Why Work With Us?

Raising the Bar for ABA Excellence

CSD is not just another ABA provider. We are the architects of possibility for children and teens with autism and ASD—and the clinicians and therapists who serve them. We stand at the intersection of compassion and evidence, where human connection meets clinical excellence.

Where Innovation Meets Passion

In an industry known for burnout and turnover, we do things differently. We care about you as much as our clients. We invest in you—the person who delivers life-changing breakthroughs on a daily basis, creating a professional community where innovation meets compassion, and your voice matters.

Advocating for Diversity in Everything We Do

As champions for neurodivergent children, we practice what we preach. Our CSD family is diverse, and our Neurodivergent Advisory Board made up of team members deeply influences how we work.

CSD's Values

Our North Star

It's not just about what you do. It's who you are and the impact you make that set you apart.

Our Mission

CSD opens a world of possibilities—by making the world possible—for children with autism and their families. We do this through compassionate, individually tailored, evidence-based care that meets you exactly where you are, guiding your child to success on an innovative, breakthrough ABA journey to successful skill building.

CSD's Guiding Principles

Resilience through support

We know this work is hard. That's why we invest in our clinicians' growth, well-being, and professional development.

Partnership over prescription

Families are the true experts about their children. Our role is to listen and co-create ABA therapy plans for every family's goals, culture, and values.

Innovation rooted in humanity

We push the boundaries of what ABA therapy can be, but never lose sight of the human moments that matter most.

Science meets heart

Data guides us. Empathy drives us. Every therapy plan is evidence-based and deeply personal.

Access as a right, not a privilege

We design our program around one question: How do we bring exceptional care to every child who needs it—regardless of where they are, who they are, or their culture?

Humility in practice

Our commitment to continuous improvement makes us better practitioners, advocates, and care providers.
