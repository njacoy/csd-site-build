Our Approach

Innovation Grounded in Empathy

We are architects of possibility. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. For too long, autism therapy has felt overwhelming and overly clinical. CSD is changing that. We stand at the intersection of compassion and evidence, where human connection meets clinical excellence.

We believe in your child—their interests, their pace, their potential. Our clinically rigorous, individually tailored ABA programs are built around what makes your child light up, not a rigid playbook. From our pioneering virtual platform to the living room or therapy center floor, we bring the science of behavior analysis to life with heart, warmth, flexibility, and an unwavering commitment to your family's success.

We bring hope, joy, and a clear roadmap to guide your family.

Our Services

Therapy Where Life Happens

Choose the setting that works best for your family—or combine them. CSD is designed to grow alongside your child.

Virtual & VR Therapy

We lead in ABA innovation with our virtual program, brought to life by gamification and VR technology—always with live onscreen guidance from your therapist. Flexible scheduling fits around classes and family routines.

FPO — Virtual Therapy Child engaged in VR therapy session

In-Home Therapy

Familiar spaces fuel confidence. Our clinicians come to you, embedding skill-building into your child's natural routines and environment, helping them integrate with family dynamics for a more cohesive home life.

FPO — In-Home Therapy Clinician + child in home setting

Center-Based Therapy

Safe, structured spaces designed for focus and growth—where your child can learn, practice, play, and progress individually and alongside peers. Parents and caregivers have support from people who understand their world.

FPO — Center-Based Therapy Bright therapy center environment

School-Based Therapy

When your school engages CSD's programs, we meet your child in the classroom, building communication and adaptive skills where they're needed most—right at school, in the flow of their daily life.
